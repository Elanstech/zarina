Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer residential property sales, rentals, and investment property management.

What is the process of buying a home?

The process involves several steps including pre-approval for a mortgage, house hunting, making an offer, and closing the deal.

What should I consider when selling my home?

Consider the market conditions, pricing your home correctly, and making necessary repairs or upgrades to attract buyers.

How do I determine my budget for buying a home?

Determine your budget by considering your income, expenses, and the amount you can afford for a down payment and monthly mortgage payments.

How do I prepare my home for sale?

Prepare your home for sale by decluttering, cleaning, making necessary repairs, and staging it to appeal to potential buyers.
