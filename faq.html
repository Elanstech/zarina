Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of real estate services including buying, selling, and rental assistance, as well as property management, market analysis, and investment advice.

How can I get a market analysis of my property?

You can request a market analysis by filling out the form on our "What's Your Home Worth?" page, or by contacting us directly. We'll assess your property and provide a detailed report.

What areas do you serve?

We serve New York City and the surrounding areas, including Queens, Brooklyn, Manhattan, and Long Island. Our expertise extends to both residential and commercial properties.

What is your commission rate?

Our commission rates are competitive and vary depending on the type of service and property. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

How do I get in touch with an agent?

You can reach out to us via our contact page, call us directly, or visit our office. Our agents are always ready to assist you with your real estate needs.

Can you help with mortgage financing?

Yes, we can connect you with trusted mortgage brokers and lenders who can help you find the best financing options for your situation.
