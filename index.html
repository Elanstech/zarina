About Us

East Coast Realty by Zarina was founded by Zarina Yusupov, a passionate real estate professional dedicated to helping clients find their dream homes and investment properties. With years of experience in the industry, Zarina has built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and personalized service.

Our company specializes in renting and selling apartments and houses in the Rego Park and Forest Hills area. We offer comprehensive solutions to ensure a seamless and successful real estate experience.

Zarina Yusupov, the founder and CEO, is known for her deep understanding of the real estate market, keen negotiation skills, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Under her leadership, East Coast Realty has grown into a trusted and respected name in the real estate industry.

At East Coast Realty, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, guided by our core values of honesty, transparency, and professionalism. Our dedicated team of experienced agents is always ready to assist you with your real estate needs, ensuring you receive the best possible service and results.
