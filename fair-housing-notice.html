Ensuring Equal Housing Opportunities

At East Coast Realty by Zarina, we are committed to ensuring that everyone has equal access to housing opportunities. We adhere to the principles of the Fair Housing Act and do not discriminate against any individual based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, or familial status.

If you believe you have been discriminated against, please contact us immediately. We take these matters very seriously and will work to address your concerns promptly and thoroughly.
