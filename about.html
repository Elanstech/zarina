Our Journey

The evolution of East Coast Realty from its founding to today

2003

East Coast Realty was founded by Zarina Yusupova with a vision to provide exceptional real estate services to the New York City community. Starting with a small office in Queens and a team of just three agents, the company began building its reputation for personalized service and market expertise.

2008

Despite the challenges of the global financial crisis, East Coast Realty not only survived but thrived by focusing on client needs and adapting to market conditions. Our expertise in helping clients navigate difficult market conditions solidified our reputation as a trusted advisor.

2013

To better serve our growing client base, East Coast Realty relocated to our current flagship office on Queens Boulevard. This expanded space allowed us to grow our team and enhance our services to include dedicated investment property consulting and property management divisions.

2018

Celebrating 15 years of excellence, East Coast Realty reached the milestone of having assisted over 1,000 families in buying or selling homes. Our commitment to community involvement also grew with the establishment of our annual neighborhood improvement initiative.

2023

East Coast Realty marked its 20th anniversary by expanding our digital presence and incorporating innovative technology solutions to enhance client experiences. Today, we continue to grow while maintaining the personalized service that has been our hallmark for two decades.
